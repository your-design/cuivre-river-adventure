## Planning Considerations

### Weather Timing

All day trips should **depart by 9 AM** to:
- Complete activities before afternoon storms
- Return to camp by dinner time
- Avoid driving mountain roads in thunderstorms

### Packing for Day Trips

Even for short trips, bring:
- Water (1 liter per person minimum)
- Snacks
- Sun protection
- Rain jacket (afternoon storms)
- Camera
- Cash (small vendors may not take cards)

## Combining with Activities

### Split the Group

Not everyone has to do everything. Consider:
- Teens to Manitou Incline while younger kids go to Florissant
- Adventure seekers to Leadville while others enjoy a camp day
- Morning at Garden of the Gods, afternoon exploring different interests in Colorado Springs

### One Full Day Trip + Camp Days

Many families find one major day trip per week is enough. The rest of the time, enjoy the park you came to visit. The day trip provides variety without exhausting the vacation.
