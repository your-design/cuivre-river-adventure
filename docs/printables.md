## 🖨️ How to print these

Each sheet opens in your browser as its own page. Use your browser's **Print**
(or **Save as PDF**) to print at home before you leave—then toss them in a folder
or on a clipboard. No internet needed once they're printed.

{: .note }
> **Print before you go!** Cell signal at camp is spotty. Print a few extra copies
> of the kids' sheets so each kid (or family) gets their own, and laminate the camp
> and meal schedules so they survive the week.
