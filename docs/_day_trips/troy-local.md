# Troy & Lincoln County
{: .no_toc }

The closest town (~15 min)—your hub for food, ice, supplies, and a couple of local stops.
{: .fs-6 .fw-300 }

---

## The Practical Stuff

Troy is our **resupply and backup base**, about 10–15 minutes from the park:

- **Groceries & Walmart** — ice refills, fresh meat/produce, anything forgotten, and
  **Missouri fishing permits**.
- **Gas** — last easy fuel before the park.
- **Pharmacy & Mercy Hospital Lincoln** — good to know where these are with kids along.
- **Restaurants** — a nice "night off" for the cooks (the Older Smiths flagged a
  **pizza night out**—Troy has options).

---

## Things to Do in Troy

- **Lincoln County Historical Museum** — small-town history, artifacts, and exhibits;
  a quick, cheap, air-conditioned stop.
- **Woods' Fort site** — a small park on the site of a War of 1812 stockade, with
  reassembled log cabins and a memorial marker.
- **City parks** — Troy's community parks have **playgrounds, ball fields, pavilions,
  and a pond with ducks**—a good leg-stretch and energy-burner for the kids.
- **Farmers market / seasonal events** — check dates; summer often has a market or fair.

---

## Make It Easy

- Pair a Troy trip with the **mid-week grocery/ice refill** so it does double duty.
- Hit a **playground + lunch out** to reset cranky kids, then back to camp for the fire.
- Verify hours before you go—small-town spots keep limited summer hours.
