## How to Use It

Each day suggests a **morning**, **midday (heat)**, **afternoon**, and **evening**
block. Mix and match across families—paddlers paddle, bikers bike, fishers fish, and
the take-it-easy crew holds down camp and the beach.

**Two rules that make it work:**
1. **Active in the cool hours** (morning & evening); **rest/water/shade midday.**
2. **Off the water at the first thunder**—storms pass, then resume.
