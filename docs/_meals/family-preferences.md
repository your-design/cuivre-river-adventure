## Zuelke Family
*2 adults, 2 kids*

- PB&J sandwiches (grape **and** strawberry)
- Hot dogs
- Watermelon
- Mac & cheese
- Bacon · eggs · hashbrowns
- Variety-pack chips
- Sweet corn on the cob
- Honeycrisp apples
- Bread · smooth peanut butter
- Corned beef · turkey
- Cheese
- **Brats** · carrots
- Beer

**Best fit in the plan:** hosts the **Wed arrival cookout** (dogs & brats) and a big
**Thursday breakfast** (bacon/eggs/hashbrowns).

## Harris Family
*1 adult, 2 kids*

**Breakfast & dairy**
- Vanilla yogurt cups (Great Value)
- Malt-O-Meal (bag) · Fruity Pebbles · Honey Nut Cheerios
- Cinnamon-swirl bagels · strawberry cream cheese
- Pillsbury cinnamon rolls
- 2% milk · scrambled eggs

**Lunch & snacks**
- PB&J (creamy PB, strawberry/grape — **no fruit chunks**)
- String cheese (mozzarella) · grilled cheese
- Cheez-Its · goldfish · baked Lays · Cheetos · Doritos (nacho cheese)
- Cinnamon applesauce

**Fruit & veg**
- Bananas · grapes · apples
- Peas · green beans · broccoli

**Dinners & drinks**
- Hamburgers · hot dogs · mac & cheese
- Chicken thighs · bacon · ham
- Sprite · Dr Pepper · water

**Best fit in the plan:** hosts **Burger Night (Thu)**; their cereal/bagel/fruit list
anchors the everyday breakfasts and kid snacks.

## Older Smiths
*2 adults*

- Hamburgers · brats/hot dogs · chicken thighs
- **Pulled pork** · beans
- Mashed potatoes · rice
- Spaghetti · salad
- Green beans · cold veggies (carrots, celery, etc.)
- Chips
- *Dinner out one night (maybe pizza — Backroads Pizza & Grill or a local spot)*

**Best fit in the plan:** leads the **July 4 BBQ** (pulled pork + chicken + beans) and
hosts **Spaghetti Night (Sun)**.

{: .note }
> They flagged a **pizza night out**. A good option is a mid-week dinner in **Troy**
> (~10–15 min away) to give the cooks a night off—see [Day Trips](/day-trips/).

## Younger Smiths
*2 adults, 1 kid*

**The signature trick:** pre-cooked meats **vacuum-sealed in sauce**, finished the
last 10–20% after thawing — steak, chicken, pork in **Mexican, tikka masala, Korean,
Italian**, etc. (See [Meal Prep](/meals/meal-prep).)

- Eggs · bacon · pancakes · biscuits & gravy
- Lunch-meat / tuna / egg sandwiches · Hawaiian bread · tortillas
- Nutella · chips · spam
- **Rice** (almost every meal) · ramen
- Mashed potatoes from **dehydrated flakes** (just add boiling water)
- Roasted chicken · hot dogs · sandwich bread
- Fruit (apples, bananas) · carrots
- Cereal (Cheerios)
- Coffee · water · milk · soda · beer

**Best fit in the plan:** hosts **Taco Night (Fri)** with the pre-seasoned meats and
brings the **dehydrated mashed potatoes** for Monday's grill-out.
