## How It Works

- **Dinner** = group meal. **One host family brings the food *and* cooks the whole dinner;**
  **two assigned people** from other families clean up.
- **Breakfast** = **6 total: 3 hot, 3 cold.**
  - **Hot (Thu, Sat, Mon):** an assigned crew **buys + cooks**; a **different** crew cleans.
  - **Cold (Fri, Sun, Tue):** **each family brings its own** cold breakfast items.
- **Lunch** = **per family, self-serve, every day.** Each family **fixes and cleans up its
  own.** Two people set food out; two put it away.
- ⭐ = make-ahead item ([Meal Prep guide](/meals/meal-prep)). 🍕 = eating **out**;
  🛒 = a **supply run** is built into that day.

Coffee station, snack pantry, and the sign-up sheets are on
[Camp Jobs & Chores](/guides/camp-jobs).

## Lunch — Every Day, Self-Serve

The same simple kit every day; pick what you want:

- **Options daily:** sandwiches · grilled cheese · mac & cheese · **leftover day**.
- **Every family fixes its own and cleans up its own.**
- **Two people set the food out; two people put it away** (sign up on the
  [Task Sign-Up sheet](/assets/printables/task-signup.html)).
- **Every family brings:** **one loaf of bread** + **one or two *large* bags of chips to
  share** (large bags, not individual—**bring chip clips** so nothing goes stale).
- **Out for the day?** Whoever isn't back at camp at lunch **packs their own.**
- **🦇 Cave day (Sun):** **everyone packs their own family lunch** for Meramec.

## Day-by-Day

### Wednesday, July 1 — Arrival 🌭
*Travel day. Keep it easy.*

- **Dinner — Brats & Hot Dogs Cookout** *(host: Zuelke)* — brats + dogs, buns,
  ketchup/mustard/relish; **Older Smiths bring a 3-bean medley.** Easy after setup.
- **After setup:** Day-1 **Troy Walmart run** for milk/yogurt and any cold-breakfast items.
- **Snacks on the road:** chips, fruit, string cheese, applesauce pouches, water.

### Thursday, July 2 — Settle In 🔥
- **Breakfast (HOT) — Big Camp Breakfast:** **19 eggs**, **bacon (2 pkg)**, large
  **hashbrowns**, **Pillsbury cinnamon rolls (4 pkg)**.
  **Buy + cook: Zuelke & Harris · Cleanup: Younger & Older Smiths.**
- **Lunch — Self-serve sandwich bar** (per family).
- **Dinner — Burgers & Dogs + Corn** *(host: Harris)* — hamburgers ⭐ + hot dogs, **sweet
  corn on the cob**, veggie tray.

### Friday, July 3 — Taco Night ❄️ 🛒
- **Breakfast (COLD) — own items** (cereal, yogurt, bagels, fruit).
- **🛒 Midday supply run:** a couple of adults run to **Troy for water, bagged ice, and
  restock** during the hottest hours.
- **Lunch — Self-serve** (grilled cheese / mac & cheese are easy wins).
- **Dinner — Taco Bar / Walking Tacos** *(host: Younger Smiths)* — ⭐ pre-cooked seasoned
  meat reheated, rice, tortillas, cheese, lettuce, salsa, sour cream; crush-the-bag
  **walking-taco** option for the kids.

### Saturday, July 4 — Independence Day 🎆 🔥
- **Breakfast (HOT) — Pancakes, Waffles, Bacon & Eggs** (go red-white-blue with berries).
  **Buy + cook: Younger Smiths · Cleanup: Zuelke & Harris** *(all hands welcome).*
- **Lunch — Light picnic + chips** (there's a feast tonight).
- **Dinner — 🎆 BBQ** *(host: Older Smiths)* — ⭐ **pulled pork** + **BBQ chicken thighs**,
  **baked beans**, **pasta salad**. Eat a bit early, then caravan to the
  **[Troy Rotary fireworks](/itinerary/day4)**.
- **Dessert:** s'mores + a red-white-blue treat.

### Sunday, July 5 — Cave Day 🦇 (pizza out)
*Big day trip to [Meramec Caverns](/day_trips/meramec-caverns) near Sullivan—stay cool underground.*

- **Breakfast (COLD) — own grab-&-go items;** coffee to-go. Eat fast to beat heat & crowds.
- **📦 Lunch — Pack your own family lunch** (sandwiches, chips, fruit) for the road/cave.
- **🍕 Dinner — Pizza OUT** on the drive back—no camp cooking. *(Spot TBD—see below.)*

### Monday, July 6 — Last Full Day 🔥
- **Breakfast (HOT) — Biscuits & Gravy, Eggs, Hashbrowns** + **bagged donuts for kids**.
  **Buy + cook + provide: Older Smiths · Cleanup: Younger Smiths** *(all hands welcome).*
- **Lunch — Self-serve; clear out leftovers.**
- **Dinner — Spaghetti, Green Beans & Salad** *(host: Older Smiths)* — ⭐ make-ahead meat
  sauce; simple, one big pot, easy cleanup. Finish the watermelon.

### Tuesday, July 7 — Departure ❄️
- **Breakfast (COLD) — pack-up:** own items; eat down what's left.
- **On the road:** leftover snacks, water.

## Tips for Feeding the Group

- **Feed little kids a few minutes early** to head off hangry meltdowns.
- **Cook once, eat twice:** make extra at dinner for next-day lunches/leftover day.
- **Host cooks, two other people clean**—keep it fair.
- Keep a **"kid plate" default** (plain version) at every dinner.
