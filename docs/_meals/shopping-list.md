## How to Use This List

- **It's finalized.** We sat down together and claimed everything — this list is now
  grouped **by store aisle**, then **by who's bringing it**, so you can shop your part fast.
- Quantities assume **~12 people (7 adults, 5 kids) for 6 days**.
- **Buy shelf-stable & frozen at home**; buy **fresh/cold and ice near the end of the
  drive** (Wentzville or Troy). See [The Drive](/guides/drive#the-last-grocery--gas-run).
- A few items are marked **"buy first night / mid-week at Walmart"** — pick those up on the
  Troy run, not at home.
- ✅ 🖨️ **Print the [Shopping List & Sign-Up sheet](/assets/printables/shopping-list.html)** —
  the same list in a print-friendly layout to carry into the store.

{: .important }
> **Every family still brings, no matter what:** **one loaf of bread** + **one or two
> *large* bags of chips to share** + **their own cold-breakfast items** (cereal, milk,
> yogurt, bagels, fruit). Bring **chip clips** so big bags don't go stale.

**Legend:** ⭐ = good to **cook/freeze ahead** · 🧊 = **buy cold/fresh near the park** (not at
home) · *Everyone* = each family brings its own.
