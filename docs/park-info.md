### Facilities

**Camper Services Building (Rocky Ridge):**
- Showers
- Flush toilets
- Laundry
- Available Memorial Day – Labor Day

**11 Mile Marina (North Shore):**
- Boat rentals
- Fishing supplies
- Snacks and firewood
- Fishing licenses

### Key Regulations

1. Display valid parks pass on each vehicle
2. Keep vehicles on established roads
3. Camp only in established sites with reservation
4. Build fires only in provided fire rings
5. Keep pets on leash (6 feet max)
6. Dispose of trash properly
7. Observe quiet hours: 10 PM – 6 AM

### Water Rules

- No swimming
- No water skiing
- No tubing or wading
- No jet skis
- No scuba diving
- Kayaking, canoeing, boating permitted
- Boating closed ½ hour after sunset to ½ hour before sunrise
