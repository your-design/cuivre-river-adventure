Lunch — Every Day, Self-Serve

The dealEach family brings
Options daily: sandwiches · grilled cheese · mac & cheese · leftovers. Every family fixes & cleans up its own. 2 set out, 2 put away. Out for the day? Pack your own. Cave day = pack your own family lunch. 1 loaf of bread + 1–2 large bags of chips to share (bring chip clips!) + your own favorite lunch extras.
Dessert all week: everyone brings a dessert item (cookies, etc.). Older Smiths: brownies, Rice Krispie treats over the fire, Little Debbies.
