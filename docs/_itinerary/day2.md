# Day 2 — Thursday, July 2
{: .no_toc }

Settle in. An easy first full day: the lake, fishing, and getting our rhythm.
{: .fs-6 .fw-300 }

---

## At a Glance

| Block | Plan |
|:------|:-----|
| Morning | Big camp breakfast; first fishing & shoreline explore |
| Midday | Beach time / shade / naps |
| Afternoon | Lakeside or short stroll; finish dialing in camp |
| Evening | Burger night; first naturalist program if offered |

---

## Morning

- **Big Camp Breakfast** (eggs, bacon, hashbrowns) — see the [schedule](/meals/meal-schedule).
- **Easy first fishing** off the bank at Lake Lincoln—bluegill are the perfect
  confidence-builder for kids. ([Fishing guide](/activities/fishing).)
- Let everyone find their feet: short walks, the [Lakeside Trail](/activities/hiking),
  scoping the beach.

## Midday — Beat the Heat

- **Beach session** at Lake Lincoln (8 a.m.–8 p.m.; swim only at the beach).
- Shade, water, snacks, and naps for the little ones. ([Swimming guide](/activities/swimming).)

## Afternoon

- Finish setting up the group HQ; build the snack/drink station.
- Optional short [hike](/activities/hiking) or creek wade in **Big Sugar Creek**.
- Set the **kid rules** as a group (buddy system, water-with-an-adult, boundaries).

## Evening

- **Dinner: Burgers & Dogs + Corn on the Cob** (Harris hosts).
- Check the amphitheater board for the **naturalist program**—a great low-key first
  evening out. ([Wildlife & programs](/activities/wildlife).)
- Campfire + first **firefly catch-and-release** at dusk.

---

{: .note }
> **Don't over-plan today.** A relaxed settle-in day pays off all week.
