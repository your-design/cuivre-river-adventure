# Day 5 — Sunday, July 5 🦇
{: .no_toc }

The big day trip: Meramec Caverns near Sullivan. Cool underground, packed family lunches, pizza out.
{: .fs-6 .fw-300 }

---

## At a Glance

| Block | Plan |
|:------|:-----|
| Early morning | Cold grab-&-go breakfast; load up, hit the road (**~1½–2 hr**) |
| Late morning | **Meramec Caverns tour** (a constant 60°F!) |
| Midday | 📦 Each family's packed lunch; skip the hottest hours underground |
| Afternoon | Gemstone sluice / river / gift shop — stay cool |
| Evening | 🍕 **Pizza out** on the drive back to camp |

---

## Why This Is *The* Day Trip

Everyone flagged the cave as a **must**, so it gets a full, scheduled day. Bonus: the
guided tour is a **constant 60°F**—the perfect way to spend the hottest day of the week
*out of the heat* instead of melting at camp. See the
[Meramec Caverns day-trip guide](/day_trips/meramec-caverns) for details.

---

## Morning — Get Rolling Early

- **Cold grab-&-go breakfast** (cereal, yogurt, fruit, bagels; coffee to-go) so we beat the
  heat and the crowds. ([Meal schedule](/meals/meal-schedule).)
- **Caravan to Meramec Caverns (~1½–2 hr)**—it's off **I-44 near Stanton/Sullivan, MO**.
- **Everyone packs their own family lunch** before we leave (cave day = self-packed).
  ([Food Plan](/meals/).)

## Late Morning — The Cave 🦇

- **Meramec Caverns guided tour** — about **1 hr 20 min**, paved, lit, level walking; great
  for all ages.
- Bring a **light layer** (it's 60°F inside) and **closed-toe shoes**.
- **Book/confirm tour times ahead** in summer — call **(573) 468-3166**.

## Midday — Lunch & Stay Cool

- **📦 Packed family lunches** (sandwiches, chips, fruit) at a shady picnic spot or by the
  Meramec River. Stay out of the 1–3 p.m. heat.
- Optional kid add-ons at the complex: **gemstone/arrowhead sluice**, the **river boat ride**,
  and the **zipline**—each its own ticket.

## Afternoon — Cool & Easy

- Linger in the cool: the sluice, the river, the gift shop, ice cream.
- Keep an eye on energy and the sky; start winding toward dinner.

## Evening — Pizza Out 🍕

- **Grab pizza on the drive back** (or back near Troy)—**no camp cooking tonight.** A
  well-earned night off for the cooks.
- Roll back to camp for a low-key campfire (or straight to bed for tired kids).

---

{: .note }
> **Heat-smart plan:** an early start + a 60°F cave + pizza out means we skip cooking *and*
> the worst of the heat. If anyone wants a slower day, a few folks can stay back at camp
> (beach, fishing, naps) while the rest do the cave.
